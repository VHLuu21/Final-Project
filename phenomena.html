Astronomical Phenomena and Upcoming Events

Throughout history, humans have gazed at the night sky in awe, captivated by eclipses, meteor showers, and rare cosmic alignments. On this page, we explore some of the most remarkable astronomical phenomena, share news from the stars, and look ahead at upcoming celestial events.

Upcoming Astronomical Events – 2025

  • March 14 Partial Solar Eclipse: Visible in Eastern Asia.
  • April 22–23 Lyrids Meteor Shower: Up to 20 meteors/hour.
  • May 6 Eta Aquarids: Especially bright in the Southern Hemisphere.
  • September 17 Penumbral Lunar Eclipse over Africa and Europe.
  • December 14 Geminids Meteor Shower: The year's brightest meteor event.
